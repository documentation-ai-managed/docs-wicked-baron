## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">
- A Facebook account with admin or editor access to at least one page.
- A modern web browser like Chrome or Firefox.
- No existing Wicked Baron account (sign up is free).
</Callout>

<Callout kind="tip">
Visit [fbaudienceanalyzer.abacusai.app](https://fbaudienceanalyzer.abacusai.app) to begin.
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Follow these steps to sign up quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Signup Page" icon="external-link">
    Open your browser and navigate to https://fbaudienceanalyzer.abacusai.app.
    
    Click the **Sign Up** button in the top right corner.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email address and create a strong password.
    
    Fill in your name and agree to the terms of service.
    
    Click **Create Account**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Wicked Baron.
    
    Click the verification link to activate your account.
    
    You'll be redirected to the login page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the site and sign in with your new credentials.
    
    You'll land on the main dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connect Your Facebook Page

Authorize Wicked Baron to access your Facebook data securely.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Connect Facebook" icon="facebook">
    On the dashboard, click **Connect Facebook Page** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Authorize Permissions" icon="shield">
    You'll be redirected to Facebook's authorization page.
    
    Review the permissions (read-only access to page insights).
    
    Click **Continue as [Your Name]**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Your Page" icon="list">
    Select the Facebook page you want to analyze from the dropdown.
    
    Click **Connect** to complete the setup.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Run Your First Audience Analysis

Generate insights for your connected page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Analysis Type" icon="search">
    From the dashboard, choose **Audience Analysis** from the top menu.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Date Range" icon="calendar">
    Pick a date range, such as the last 30 days.
    
    Click **Run Analysis**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Results" icon="check-circle">
    Wait 10-30 seconds for processing.
    
    View charts and tables summarizing your audience data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Metrics to Review

Focus on these essential metrics right away:

| Metric              | Description                                      | Why It Matters                          |
|---------------------|--------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------|
| Follower Growth     | Net new followers over time                      | Tracks organic and paid growth          |
| Top Locations       | Countries/cities with most engagement            | Target geographic content               |
| Age & Gender        | Audience demographics breakdown                  | Tailor messaging to your core users     |
| Engagement Rate     | Likes, comments, shares per post                 | Measures content resonance              |
| Peak Active Times   | Hours/days with highest activity                 | Schedule posts for maximum reach        |
