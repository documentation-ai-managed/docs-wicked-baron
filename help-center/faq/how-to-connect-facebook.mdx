## Overview

RankRocket ContentIQ requires you to connect your Facebook account to analyze your pages.

## Steps

1. Log in to [RankRocket ContentIQ](https://fbaudienceanalyzer.abacusai.app)
2. Go to the dashboard and click "Connect Facebook"
3. Sign in with your Facebook credentials
4. Authorize the requested permissions
5. Select the page(s) you want to analyze

You should now see your Facebook data in the dashboard.
