---
title: Dashboard Not Loading
description: Fix RankRocket ContentIQ dashboard loading issues.
---

## Symptoms

- White screen on login
- Stuck at loading spinner

## Causes

- Browser cache issues
- Blocked scripts/extensions

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clear your browser cache">
    ````
    Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac) to hard reload.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Disable browser extensions">
    Try incognito mode or disable ad/script blockers.
  </Step>
</Steps>
