## Overview

Export key analytics from Wicked Baron for reports or presentations.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your dashboard">
    Log in and select the Facebook page you want to export data from.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Export Insights'">
    On the top right, select the export button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose format">
    Select CSV or XLSX.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download the file">
    File downloads to your device automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>
